Essentials Team Builder

Essentials Team Builder is a tool designed to help make the development of Pokémon fan-games easier, by providing an easy-to-use GUI. Just fill out the required fields, click the "Generate/Preview" button, and you are done!

This is a fan-made tool. Pokémon is not owned by TheCrafters001; Pokémon is owned by GAME FREAK Inc. This tool does not allow you to create your own fan-games, rather it is to help aid you.

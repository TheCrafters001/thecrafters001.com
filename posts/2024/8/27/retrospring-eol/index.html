Retrospring - End of Life

August 27, 2024 at 2:48 AM EDT

Talking about the end of Retrospring

Retrospring, a way to ask questions to users for them to answer, is getting shut down on the 1st of March 2025. I used Retrospring to answer a few questions ask to me, whether it was from an anon user, or a user I knew, I tried to answer them. However, with the light of the closure of Retrospring in the future, I plan to find another way for others to ask questions to me. Right now, I have a pretty much fresh Tumblr account I don't use for much, I have opened up questions there, so if you want, you can ask me questions there for now. Here is the link: https://www.tumblr.com/new/ask/thecrafters001

Until Retrospring shuts down, my shortlink I made (https://thecrafters001.github.io/links/ask) will remain up. After the shutdown, however, it will be changed to link to this blog post, tumblr, or whatever other service I find. I know Tumblr isn't the best option, as you need to create an account, even to ask as anon, something Retrospring didn't require, so I will be looking for alternatives. I will update this blog post later if I do end up finding a new service and link that here.

Until then, you can keep asking me questions on Retrospring until the service gets shut down.
